üí° Suggestions: Additional Quiz Questions

Based on the diagnostic framework and typical Route One client needs, here are recommended questions to consider for future iterations:

Revenue Tier Question

"What's your approximate annual revenue?"
Options: <$1M, $1-5M, $5-20M, $20-50M, $50M+
Value: Enables tier-based pricing and service recommendations. Higher revenue = higher impact messaging.

Current Solution Question

"How are your financials currently handled?"
Options: In-house bookkeeper, External accountant, Outsourced firm, DIY/Founder, Hybrid
Value: Positions Route One against current solution and tailors the case study shown.

Decision Timeline Question

"When are you looking to improve your financial operations?"
Options: Immediately, This quarter, This year, Just exploring
Value: Prioritizes hot leads for immediate outreach vs. nurture sequences.

Team Size / Growth Stage

"What best describes your company right now?"
Options: Solo founder, Small team (2-10), Growth stage (11-50), Scaled (50+)
Value: Adjusts complexity messaging and solution positioning.

Note: Each additional question adds ~15 seconds to completion time. Recommend testing with 1-2 new questions at a time to measure impact on completion rates.
