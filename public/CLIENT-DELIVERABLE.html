What This Does

People land on the quiz, answer 6 questions about their financial operations, and get a personalized score. To see their full results, they enter their email. You get the lead.

The flow:

  1. Visitor sees headline, clicks "Start Quiz"
  2. 6 quick questions (takes about 90 seconds)
  3. They enter name, email, company to see results
  4. They get a maturity score (5-95) with personalized insights
  5. You get their info + answers in your Google Sheet + email notification

Where Your Leads Go

Every time someone completes the quiz, two things happen:

  1. Google Sheet — Their info, answers, and score get added automatically
  2. Email — You get a notification at dean@routeoneadvisory.com

The sheet includes everything you need to follow up: name, email, company, what they answered, their score, and when they took it.
