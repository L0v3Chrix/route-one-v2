What It Does

Someone lands on the page, takes a 90-second quiz about their financial operations, and gets a personalized score. To see their results, they give you their email.

You get their contact info, every answer they gave, and their score — automatically in your Google Sheet and in your inbox.

That's it. Simple by design.

But Here's the Real Thing

This quiz isn't the point. It's a proof of concept.

What we actually built is a system. A way to turn your expertise into something that works while you sleep. Something that qualifies leads before you ever get on a call. Something that scales.

This is one funnel. Imagine ten of them — each speaking to a different industry, a different pain point, a different entry point into Route One. All feeding the same pipeline. All running automatically.

That's the door this opens.
